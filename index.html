FAQ

How long does tint take to cure?

Curing varies by weather. You may see slight haze or small water pockets early on. We’ll give you a simple care guide.

Do you help me choose the right film?

Yes. We’ll recommend based on heat rejection goals, visibility preferences, and budget.

Can you match factory privacy glass?

We can get very close depending on the vehicle and film selection. We’ll walk through options before installation.

What do you need from me for a quote?

Vehicle year/make/model, service type, and preferences (shade/coverage). Photos help for custom requests.
